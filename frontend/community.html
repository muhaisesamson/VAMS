Veteran Support

Your Service to Uganda Will Always Be Valued

Your sacrifice, discipline and commitment helped protect our nation. Although military service may have ended, your contribution to Uganda remains an important part of our national history.

This platform is intended to connect veterans with support groups, welfare initiatives and community programmes that can provide encouragement, guidance and companionship during life after service.
